ABOUT US

ABES Assist is a smart, student-centric platform built to simplify and enhance campus life at ABES. By integrating AI, blockchain, and practical tools, it addresses real student needs — from secure logins and academic help to GPA tracking and real-time peer support. It creates a more connected, informed, and stress-free student experience.

ABES Assist

The idea behind ABES Assist arises from the everyday challenges faced by students in academic and campus life. From scattered resources to lack of real-time guidance, students often struggle to find quick, reliable support. ABES Assist combines smart technologies to offer a unified platform tailored to these real needs.

Idea

The motivation for ABES Assist stems from the everyday struggles students face—uncertainty during exams, last-minute resource needs, and a lack of centralized support. It is driven by the goal to provide reliable, student-friendly solutions that enhance academic life and reduce stress.

Motivation

At ABES Assist, we envision a campus where every student has instant access to the tools, guidance, and support they need. Our mission is to simplify academic life, foster peer connections, and move toward a more student-friendly future.

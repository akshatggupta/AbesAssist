Things to Remember While Uploading an Image


Image Description: Ensure the image clearly represents the sample to be tested.

Positioning: Position the sample in the center of the frame for accurate results.

File Size: Keep the image file size under 2 MB to avoid upload issues.

Accuracy: Upload only images that truly represent the actual sample being tested.

Clarity and Quality: Ensure the image is clear, well-lit, and free from any obstructions.

Additional Requirements: Follow any specific guidelines provided by the medical website or facility.
